Instructions:
Each player starts with 20HP (Health).
Player draws random hand size on hand will be 10 cards
Player plays a card from hand to the battlefield
Select monster form the battlefield is selected to attack and defending player selects a card to defend is one is present
Each card has a Strength score this represents attack and a toughness score this represents defense
When attacking is stronger than defence the weaker monsters are destroyed and the difference is converted into HP damage
Game ends when one player's health is dropped to 0.
